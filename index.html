Overview

Resynth is a modification to Minecraft that focuses on giving the player a way to obtain every in-game resource through plants. It ties directly into the game so you obtain the resouces and seeds just by mining - no need to do anything special... except blow up some TNT here and there.

From the beginning Resynth was designed to be completely configurable, so everything from plant growth times to seed drop rates to ore generation can be changed in the config file.

Collecting Seeds

Seeds are obtained differently depending on the plant and ore.

Seeds for diamond, coal, emerald, glowstone, quartz, redstone and Lapis Lazuli are obtained by mining the respective ore block (e.g. diamond ore and glowstone) with an iron pickaxe or better. If you're lucky, seeds will randomly drop .

Seeds for iron and gold are obtained by blowing up the ore block. If you're feeling lucky, the respective seeds will occasionally drop .

Slow Plant Growth

Resynth plants grow quite slowly compared to vanilla minecraft plants on average .

The plants growth rate is also affected by the mineral soil block it is placed on. Each mineral soil block has a mineral content - this value affects how fast the plants grow. When placed in the world, the mineral soil block has a mineral content of 1%, which can go up to 50%. Plants below 25% will grow very slowly and plants of around 1% will seamingly never grow.

The mineral content can be increase by right clicking the soil block with either Mineral Rocks (1% increase ) or Dense Mineral Rocks (9% increase )

Getting the resource (I want my diamonds)

The produce from the plant (organic ore and straw) can be placed in a furnaced to get the Minecraft resource (such as diamond).
