All The Resources!

Grow all the resources you want - from ores to mobs to mods. Change all the settings - drops, growth times, rarity, difficulty and more. Different plants for different resources, all with different mechanics. Support for other mods: Applied Energistics 2, Tinkers' Construct, Forestry.

Growable Ores, Resources and Mob Drops

Completely Configurable

Resynth focuses on three things:

  1. Provide a way to grow every resource (ores, mob drops, modded resources and other miscellaneous resources) with some variety and mechanics.

  2. Allow you to decide the difficulty.

  3. Integrate tightly into the game - no need to go out of your way to get seeds or resources.

Resynth adds 3 different plant types to Minecraft which grow different types of resource:

  1. Crystalline - For ores that don't need smelting (Minecraft Wheat Equivalent)

  2. Metallic - For ores that do need smelting (Minecraft Melon Equivalent)

  3. Biochemical - For mob drops

Seeds are obtained differently depending on the plant type - For the crystalline and biochemical plants, seeds are obtained simply by mining and killing mobs. Metallic plant seeds are obtained by blowing the ore block up. A plant only ever drops one seed.

A new mechanic - Resynth plants grow a bit differently when compared to vanilla Minecraft plants. They require a special block to be planted, Mineral Soil, which has a distinct property - a mineral content percentage. This value controls how fast the plant grows. Plants growing on mineral soil with a low mineral content will grow extremely slowly, while plants growing on mineral soil with a high mineral content will grow quite quickly.
