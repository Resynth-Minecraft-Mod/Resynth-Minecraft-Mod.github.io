Grow All The Ores!

Resynth adds three new plants with new mechanics that give you a way to grow all the ores, mob drops and resources in both vanilla and modded Minecraft.
Change and configure Resynth to your liking - play it the way you want to play it!
Peaceful mode friendly.

The Resynth Minecraft Mod

Completely Configurable

Resynth focuses on three things:

  1. Provide a way to grow every resource (ores, mob drops, modded resources and other miscellaneous resources) with some variety and mechanics.

  2. Allow you to decide the difficulty - Change almost any setting to make Resynth fit your play style.

  3. Integrate tightly into the game - no need to go out of your way to get seeds or resources. No complicated crafting recipes or time consuming grinding for some seeds.

The three plants for the three resources:

  1. Crystalline Plants - For ores that don't need smelting (similar to Minecraft wheat plants).

  2. Metallic Plants - For ores that do need smelting (similar to Minecraft melon plants).

  3. Biochemical Plants - For mob drops (no similar Minecraft plant).

Growing the resources

Seeds are obtained differently depending on the plant type - For the crystalline and biochemical plants, seeds are obtained simply by mining and killing mobs. Metallic plant seeds are obtained by blowing the ore block up. A plant only ever drops one seed when broken.

Resynth plants grow a bit differently when compared to vanilla Minecraft plants. They require a special block to be planted, Mineral Soil, which has a distinct property - a mineral content percentage. This value controls how fast the plant grows. Plants growing on mineral soil with a low mineral content will grow extremely slowly, while plants growing on mineral soil with a high mineral content will grow quite quickly.
