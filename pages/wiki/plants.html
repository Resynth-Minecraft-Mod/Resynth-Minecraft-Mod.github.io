Plants
This page lists all the resources that Resynth can grow, as well as the mod the resource is from, and the type of plant that grows the resource. This page only lists resources that can be grown in the latest version of Resynth. Any resources in older versions that can no longer be grown are not listed here.

Don't see the mod, ore, crop, or resource you want to grow? New plants are constantly being added to Resynth, and more and more mods are being supported - most often from requests by players of the mod. Additionally, you can submit a plant request stating the ores/resources you want to be able to grow. View the guide on submitting plant requests.

Growable ores and resources from other mods.
The latest version of Resynth adds plants for ores and resources from the following mods in addition to the standard crops for Vanilla Minecraft resources: Basic Nether Ores Botania Blue Power Mekanism More Ores in ONE Mystical Agriculture Just Another Ruby Mod Simple Ores The Midnight
