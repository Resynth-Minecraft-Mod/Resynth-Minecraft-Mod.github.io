Mod Integration
From the beginning, Resynth has made a large attempt to support other mods. This means plants for ores, resource,s and mob drops from other mods as well as general integration. However, mod integration between different versions is quite spotty at best, so it can change quite drastically.

This page attempts to give a rundown of what mods each version supports.
Resynth Mod Integration
Ruby Seeds for Ruby gems added by other mods.

Hwyla/Waila & The One Probe Integration
Resynth adds support for both Hwyla (formally Waila) and The One Probe. However, not all versions support both mods. What information the tooltip gives also depends on the version of Resynth.


Hwyla and The One Probe tooltips for Mineral Soil in Minecraft 1.12.2.

Minecraft 1.14.4 & 1.15.2
Support for Hwyla was added back to Resynth in version 3.0.0 for Minecraft 1.14.4. Support for The One Probe has yet to be added.

Minecraft 1.13.2
Resynth does not have support for other mods in the 1.13.2 versions.

Minecraft 1.12.2
Support for Hwyla was added to Resynth in version 1.3.1 for Minecraft 1.12.2. Support for The One Probe was added in version 1.3.3.

Supported Ores & Resources from Other Mods
Resynth does allow growing resources and ores from other mods, however, it too, is spotty. This section gives the complete list of what plants are available in which Minecraft version. For a complete list of all plants in Resynth, check out these pages: List of Biochemical plants, List of Crystalline plants, List of Metallic plants.
