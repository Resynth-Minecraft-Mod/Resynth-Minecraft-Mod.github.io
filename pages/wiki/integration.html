Hwyla/Waila & The One Probe Integration
Resynth adds support for both Hwyla (formally Waila) and The One Probe tooltips. Regardless of which mod used, the experience should be the same.

Currently, each tooltip will give information about the growth stage/growth percentage of a plant as well as the mineral content and tier (called stage) of Mineral Soil blocks.
