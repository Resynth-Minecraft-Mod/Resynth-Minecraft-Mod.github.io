Dense Mineral Rock
Dense Mineral Rocks are the compressed form of Mineral Rocks. They're crafted with 9 Mineral Rocks in a crafting table. They can be added to Mineral Soil to increase the Mineral Content by the same amount as 9 Mineral Rocks.

Apart from the above uses, Dense Mineral Rocks are also used as a crafting component for Mineral Crystals and the Mineral Hoe.

Use as a Fertilizer
Like Mineral Rocks, Dense Mineral Rocks can also be used as a fertilizer. The only difference being, Dense Mineral Rocks will increase the mineral content by the same amount as nine Mineral Rocks. E.g. if one Mineral Rock would increase the mineral content by 1%, one Dense Mineral Rock will increase the mineral content by 9%
Warning: If adding Dense Mineral Rocks to Mineral Soil would increase the mineral content above the maximum (50%), the excess will be lost.
