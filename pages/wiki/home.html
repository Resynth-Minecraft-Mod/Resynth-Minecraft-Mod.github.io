Resynth Wiki!

The official Wiki for Resynth - Growable Ores and Resource. A complete guide to everything from getting started, to general tips and tricks, a list of plants and items, and every other concept in the mod.


The various resources are grown from 3 separate plant types: Crystalline for growable item drops and resources, Metallic for growable ore and other blocks, and finally, Biochemical for growable mob drops. Apart from providing some visual variety, the plant type also determines how and where the seeds are obtained, how the plant is harvested, how fast the ores or resource grows and a bunch more.

Unlike the norm, Resynth Seeds are not crafted. Rather, they are obtained by performing actions in the world, such as killing mobs to get seeds for Biochemical plant types, or blowing up ore blocks to get seeds for Metallic plant types.

Another cool feature some of you may love, is the Mystical Seed Pod. A special type of flower that spawns randomly in the world that can be configured to drop random Biochemical mob drop Seeds for players who like to play in peaceful mode.
